Adding Gers Fixtures to Your Calendar

Follow these steps to add up-to-date fixtures to your Google Calendar, and stays updated.

Important! Do this on your computer - won't work on your phone

  1. Copy the URL for the Sky Sports Rangers Calendar: http://www.skysports.com/calendars/football/fixtures/teams/rangers
  2. Go to Google Calendar on your computer.
  3. Click on '+' symbol next to Other calendars then choose From URL
  4. Paste the URL for the calendar into the URL of calendar box and click Add calendar
  5. Viola! You are now subscribed to the live calendar!
